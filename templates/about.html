Our Story

Kikky Hairs was born from a passion for helping women feel beautiful, confident, and effortless. We source only the finest 100% human hair from Vietnam and China, ensuring every strand meets our gold standard for softness, durability, and natural movement.

What started as a small studio in Lagos has grown into a trusted name across Nigeria, serving queens who refuse to compromise on quality.

Meet the Founder

Kikky, the visionary behind the brand, believes that luxury hair should be accessible to every woman. Her hands‑on approach ensures every unit that leaves our studio is perfect – from the lace to the tips.

Our Promise

  • ✅ 100% human hair – no synthetics, no blends.
  • ✅ Ethically sourced and hand‑crafted.
  • ✅ Tangle‑free, minimal shedding, lasts for months.
  • ✅ Free delivery in Lagos – fast shipping nationwide.
