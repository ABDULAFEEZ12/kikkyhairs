Our Story

Where luxury meets affordability — KikkyHair was born to empower every queen with premium 100% human hair without the premium price tag.

Rooted in Excellence

Founded in Lagos, Nigeria, KikkyHair began with a simple vision: to make high-quality, ethically sourced human hair accessible to every woman who desires confidence and elegance. We travel directly to Vietnam and China to handpick every strand, ensuring only the softest, most durable textures reach you.

Our name, "Kikky," represents boldness and authenticity. We are not just a wig brand — we are a movement celebrating natural beauty, versatility, and self-expression.

Our Core Values

Uncompromising Quality

Every bundle is inspected for softness, shine, and longevity. We never compromise on what makes you look and feel your best.

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with suppliers who treat their workers fairly. Our hair is 100% human, ethically collected.

Fast & Reliable Delivery

Same-day dispatch in Lagos, nationwide shipping within 2–5 days. Your hair arrives when you need it.

Customer Love

Our support team is real, human, and always ready to help you choose the perfect unit.
